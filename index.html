Trust & safety

A safer place to list and connect

We provide the listing and communication tools. Any arrangement for an item is made directly between users, off our platform.

Click to sell provides listing, discovery, and chat tools. We do not process payments for items between users. Our only online payments are for digital seller services — boosts, featured placement, and subscriptions.
