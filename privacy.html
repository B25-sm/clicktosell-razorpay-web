1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Click to sell ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, shares and protects your personal data when you use the Click to sell application and website (the "Platform"). We are committed to handling your data responsibly and in accordance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 and the Information Technology Act, 2000 and rules thereunder.

2. Information we collect

  • Account information: name, mobile number, email address and password.
  • Profile and listing data: photos, descriptions, prices and locations you choose to add.
  • Communications: messages you exchange with other users through the Platform and with our support team.
  • Device and usage data: device type, operating system, app version, IP address and how you interact with the Platform.
  • Location data: approximate or precise location (with your permission) to show listings near you.
  • Transaction data: records of paid services you purchase. Full payment card details are handled by our payment gateway and are not stored by us.

3. How we use your information

  • To create and manage your account and display your listings;
  • To connect buyers and sellers and enable in-app communication;
  • To show relevant listings based on category and location;
  • To process payments for paid services;
  • To detect, prevent and address fraud, abuse and security issues;
  • To provide customer support and respond to your requests;
  • To send service-related and, where permitted, promotional communications; and
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. Legal basis and consent

We process your personal data on the basis of your consent and as necessary to provide our services and meet our legal obligations. You may withdraw your consent at any time by contacting us, though this may limit your ability to use certain features.

5. How we share information

  • With other users: your public profile and listing details are visible to other users; your contact number is shared only as you choose.
  • With service providers: hosting, analytics, communication and payment partners (including Razorpay) who process data on our behalf under appropriate safeguards.
  • For legal reasons: where required by law, regulation, legal process or a governmental request, or to protect rights, safety and property.

We do not sell your personal data.

6. Payment information

Payments for paid services are processed by PCI-DSS compliant third-party gateways. We do not collect or store your complete card or banking credentials on our servers.

7. Cookies and similar technologies

We and our partners use cookies and similar technologies on our website to keep you signed in, remember preferences and understand usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

8. Data retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes and enforce our agreements. You may request deletion as described below.

9. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you have the right to access, correct, update or request deletion of your personal data, and to withdraw consent. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@clicktosell.co.in. You may also raise concerns with our Grievance Officer (see the Contact page).

10. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, alteration or disclosure. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Children

The Platform is not intended for anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with data, please contact us so we can remove it.

12. Third-party links

The Platform may contain links to third-party sites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties; please review their policies separately.

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

14. Contact us

For any privacy questions or requests, email support@clicktosell.co.in or write to Click to sell, A321 Mastermind 4, Royal Palms, Goregaon, Mumbai 400065.
